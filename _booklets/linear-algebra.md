This booklet provides a comprehensive introduction to linear algebra, covering the essential concepts that form the foundation of advanced mathematics and its applications in science and engineering.

## Course Overview

Linear algebra is the branch of mathematics concerning linear equations, linear functions, and their representations through matrices and vector spaces. This course will build your understanding from basic vector operations to advanced topics like eigenvalues and linear transformations.

## Prerequisites

- Basic algebra and trigonometry
- Familiarity with coordinate geometry
- Elementary understanding of functions

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:
- Understand vector operations and vector spaces
- Master matrix operations and properties  
- Analyze linear transformations and their matrix representations
- Apply linear algebra concepts to real-world problems
