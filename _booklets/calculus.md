This booklet introduces the fundamental concepts of single-variable calculus, providing a solid foundation for advanced mathematics, physics, and engineering applications.

## Course Overview

Calculus is the mathematical study of continuous change. This course covers the three pillars of calculus: limits, derivatives, and integrals, along with their applications to real-world problems.

## Prerequisites

- Solid understanding of algebra and trigonometry
- Knowledge of functions and their graphs
- Basic coordinate geometry

## Learning Objectives

Upon completion, students will:
- Master the concept of limits and continuity
- Understand derivatives and their applications
- Learn integration techniques and the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
- Apply calculus to solve optimization and related rate problems
